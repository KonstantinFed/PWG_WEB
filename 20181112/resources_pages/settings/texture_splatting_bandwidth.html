texture_splatting_bandwidth

Type: float
Section in INI file: Textures
Parameter in INI file: splatting_bandwidth
Default value: 0.5f

Part of texture that will be splatted with neighbour. 0.0f means no splatting, 0.5f means that 0.5 texture will be splatted for any neighbour micro chunk. Must be in [0.0f, 0.5f]???
