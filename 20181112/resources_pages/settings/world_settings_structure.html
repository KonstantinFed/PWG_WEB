Type: uint32_t
Section in INI file: [General]
Parameter in INI file: macro_chunck_size

Length of macro chunk [in points]. Used in river generation. Less values means more detail, but less performance. Must be power of 2 and more than length of micro chunk.

Type: uint32_t
Section in INI file: [General]
Parameter in INI file: micro_chunck_size
Default value: 32

Length of micro chunk [in points]. Defines smaller size of one biome place. Less values means more detail, but less performance. Must be power of 2 and less than length of macro chunk.
