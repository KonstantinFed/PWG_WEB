mountains_size

Type: std::vector<uint32_t>
Section in INI file: Mountains
Parameter in INI file: size
Default value: std::vector<uint32_t>(mountains_count)

Mountain sampling frequency [in points]. Mountain map will be square and contain size*size points. Must be power of 2.
