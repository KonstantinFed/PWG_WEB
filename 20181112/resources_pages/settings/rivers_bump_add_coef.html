rivers_bump_add_coef

Type: std::vector<float>
Section in INI file: Rivers
Parameter in INI file: bump_add_coef
Default value: std::vector<float>(rivers_count)

Coefficient for bump high points of river with different strength than low points. 1.0f means the same strenght for low and high types of points.
